// bio

Robot Fantôme is the indie / punk / rock project of Luiz Wagner Mestrinho — a Brazilian-born, Orlando-based musician, builder, and community organizer. Shoegaze noise and protest songs, DIY to the bone: the stages are built from reclaimed pallets, the merch is upcycled, and the debut EP stolenghost recordings (2022) is on every streaming platform.

Live highlights include CityArts’ Third Thursdays in downtown Orlando (in collaboration with Rooted Solutions, with Nelson Reyes on percussion), Iron Cow, and a monthly multi-genre residency at Uncle Lou’s. Off stage, he sings first bass with the Bach Festival Society of Winter Park.
